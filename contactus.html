About Fuel Buddy

Fuel Buddy was founded in 2015 with a vision to revolutionize fuel delivery services. Our mission is to provide safe, convenient, and reliable fuel refilling solutions to our customers, eliminating the need for last-minute gas station visits. Over the years, we have grown into a trusted brand serving thousands of customers nationwide.
